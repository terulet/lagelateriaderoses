Ice cream shop in Roses

Artisan Italian gelato in Roses

La Gelateria de Roses is an artisan ice cream shop in the centre of Roses, on the Costa Brava. We make Italian gelato every day with 50+ flavours, crêpes, waffles, milkshakes, smoothies and slush drinks — perfect after the beach or while walking through Roses.
